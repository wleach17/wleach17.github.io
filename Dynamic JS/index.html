Simon Says

This game requires patience and mental fortitude

Rules of the game:

  1. Wait for each button to light up then dim down before pressing button.
  2. The sequence must be pressed correctly in order to keep playing.
  3. If any mistake is made, the buttons will flash, and the game will be over.
  4. Have fun!

History

The digital arcade game of Simon Says started simply as a children's game with a leader, being reffered to as Simon, gives commands for everyone else to follow. Each person must follow the commands, exactly as the leader does them, or the person is eliminated.

Later, this game was made into an electronic game where the player copies a sequence of colors that light up, by pressing the appropiate button colors. The objective is to try and achieve the largest sequence as possible, without messing up the pattern.
